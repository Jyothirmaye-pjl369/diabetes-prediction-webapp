Understanding Diabetes & Health Assessment

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition where your body cannot properly process blood sugar (glucose), leading to high blood sugar levels that can cause serious health complications.

Risk Factors

Age, family history, obesity, high blood pressure, and sedentary lifestyle are major risk factors for developing Type 2 diabetes.
